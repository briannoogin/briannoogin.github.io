ArcAngel (2018)

Android app that aggregated and mapped tweets onto a heat map to show location of interest for first-responders. Tweets were collected through a Matlab implementation of the Twitter API. Developed with Matlab, Java, and Esri API. Developed at TAMUHACK 2018. Won Best Use of ESRI API.

GitHub

C-ASL (2018)

Utilized a convolutional neural network (CNN) and mean-shift to classify the ASL alphabet except for J and Z. Mean-shift tracks the hand with a bounding box by passing an initial calibration image. Through the bounding box, a cropped image of the hand is obtained. The cropped hand is then sent to the CNN which was trained on hand images. The CNN then outputs the most likely letter. Developed with Python, Keras, and OpenCV. Developed for ACM Projects and won second place.

Breast Cancer Diagnosis (2016)

Used an ensemble of shallow neural networks to classify whether a tumor is benign or malignant. Used statistical methods such as non-parametric bootstrapping to improve model perfomance. Used performance measures such as Aikake information criterion (AIC) and area under curve (AUC) to assess model performance. Data collected from University of Wisconsin. Developed with Matlab. Project conducted at University of Texas at Dallas.

Ginger Rail (2016)

Used an Arduino, servos, and a 2 L soda bottle to make a bottle rocket launcher. The servos enabled the launcher to move based on joystick commands. The Arduino controlled an mechanism that had a metal pin for launching the rocket launcher. When a launch command is activated by the controls, the pin punctures the cap of the bottle is punctured, and the bottle is launched. Project was a proof of concept of teaching computer science principles through low-cost materials. Arduino Project created at HackDFW.
